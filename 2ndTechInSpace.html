Orbit Around Earth


Advancement of April 12, 1961

Yuri Gagarin was the first human to orbit earth. Gagarin had accomplished this extraordinary mission on the Vostok 1 capsule. Sergei Korolev was the brilliant mind behind the Vostok 1 capsule, and ultimately used his brilliant engineering to get Gagarin to orbit the earth. This expedition had caught the world by surpirse, as it was an unexpected accomplishment. Sergei inspired many other engineers and scientists, leading the world to further great missions.
