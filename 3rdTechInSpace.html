HOW FAR WE HAVE COME

Let's reminisce in our accomplishments

From our first space mission in 1957, released as Sputnik 1, to our most recent missions in 2021. The human kind has developed so much with technology, that now we are even exploring places that we never thought we'd reach to. Although many tried to stop us, some tried to shut us down, we still found our ways to take our knowledge into the beyond. Lets hope, that in the near fututre, we will still be exploring the places nobody knows about. It is our duty, as humans, to solve problems that were never our issue in the first place.

Who To Thank


Not only are there engineers and scientist putting in their hard efforts to explore the sciences of space, but there are also brave astronauts that are willing to explore in the unknow, knowing that they could lose their lives at any minute. Yes, it is important to support those behind the scenes, but never forget to thank the heroes willing to proceed with the missions. Remember that every astronaut is just as important as any other engineer or scientist.
